Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our membership programs.

How do membership payments work?

Memberships are billed monthly on the same date you sign up. You can pay by credit card, and we offer automatic billing for convenience. Cancel anytime with 30 days notice.

What happens if I miss a month?

Unused services roll over for up to 2 months, giving you flexibility in your schedule. After that, unused services expire, but your membership benefits continue.

Can I upgrade or downgrade my membership?

Yes! You can upgrade at any time and the changes take effect immediately. Downgrades take effect at your next billing cycle to ensure you get full value.

Are there any contracts or commitments?

No long-term contracts! Our memberships are month-to-month. We're confident you'll love the value and convenience of being a member.

Can I share my membership with friends or family?

Memberships are individual and non-transferable. However, we offer referral rewards and family discounts for multiple memberships.
