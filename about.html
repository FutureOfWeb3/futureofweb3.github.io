About Lash'd Out

Our Story & Mission

Founded in 2018, Lash'd Out was born from a passion for empowering individuals through professional beauty services. We believe that everyone deserves to feel confident and beautiful, and we're here to make that happen through our expert esthetician services.

Located in the heart of North Florida, our studio provides a serene and luxurious environment where you can relax and transform. We specialize in advanced lash extension techniques, precision brow artistry, and rejuvenating facial treatments using only the finest products and equipment.

At Lash'd Out, our commitment goes beyond just providing services – we focus on building lasting relationships with our clients, understanding their unique needs, and delivering personalized care that exceeds expectations every single time.

Meet Haylee Saylor

Licensed & Insured Esthetician & Studio Owner

With over 8 years of experience in the beauty industry, Haylee Saylor is a licensed and insured esthetician passionate about helping clients achieve their beauty goals. Her expertise in lash artistry and advanced skincare techniques has made Lash'd Out a premier destination for professional beauty services.

Haylee's approach combines technical precision with artistic flair, ensuring each client receives personalized care tailored to their unique features and preferences.
