Frequently Asked Questions

How long does shipping take?

We offer several shipping options:

  • Standard Shipping: 3-5 business days
  • Express Shipping: 1-2 business days
  • Overnight Shipping: Next business day

Free shipping is available on orders over $75!

Are your Pokemon cards authentic?

Yes! All of our Pokemon cards are 100% authentic and sourced directly from official distributors. We guarantee the authenticity of every card we sell. If you ever receive a card that isn't authentic, we'll provide a full refund.

What is your return policy?

We offer a 30-day return policy for unopened products. Items must be in their original condition and packaging. Opened booster packs and single cards cannot be returned unless they arrive damaged. Please contact us at Support@rippoke.com to initiate a return.

Do you offer Pokemon card grading services?

While we don't offer grading services directly, we can recommend trusted grading companies like PSA and BGS. We also sell pre-graded cards from these services. Contact us for more information about graded card availability.

How do I track my order?

Once your order ships, you'll receive a tracking number via email. You can use this number to track your package on the carrier's website. If you have any issues with tracking, please email Support@rippoke.com with your order number.

Do you buy Pokemon cards?

We're always interested in purchasing high-value Pokemon cards and collections. Please email us at Support@rippoke.com with photos and details of what you'd like to sell. We'll get back to you with a fair offer within 24 hours.
